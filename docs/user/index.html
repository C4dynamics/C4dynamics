C4Dynamics—a powerful framework designed for algorithm development of dynamic systems.

This selected examples demonstrate the framework’s versatility, showcasing its use in diverse fields such as object detection and tracking systems, six degrees of freedom simulation, and the estimation of variables for radar-tracked targets.

Explore these real-world scenarios to witness how C4Dynamics provides sensors and objects in space and time, empowering you to develop algorithms effectively in your projects.
